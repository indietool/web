Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Which platforms are supported?

Currently macOS and Linux only.

‚ùì Where are my secrets stored?

Encrypted locally at ~/.config/indietool/secrets/, using an encryption key stored in your OS keyring (Keychain on macOS, gnome-keyring on Linux).

‚ùì What if I lose my computer?

Secrets are useless without your OS user account + keyring. Just run indietool secrets init on your new machine and re-add your secrets.

‚ùì Which registrars are supported?

Cloudflare, Porkbun, Namecheap, and Godaddy are currently supported. Others coming soon

‚ùì Can I sync secrets across machines?

No, secrets are not synced across machines. This keeps them secure and local to your development environment.

Users will have to find their own way to transfer secrets between machines if desired.

‚ùì Is there a web UI or GUI?

No, indietool is CLI-only for now. This keeps it fast, lightweight, and perfect for developer workflows.

Get Started in Minutes
